Cyber Threat Intelligence


Overview

Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) is the practice of collecting, analyzing, and applying information about potential or current cyber threats targeting your organization. CTI goes beyond technical alerts — it provides contextual insights that enable informed decision-making, proactive defense, and strategic threat mitigation.

What I Offer

I deliver actionable threat intelligence tailored to your business and industry. Services include:

Threat Actor Profiling
Dark Web and Surface Web Monitoring
Indicators of Compromise (IOC) Collection & Correlation
Threat Feed Integration & Analysis
Strategic, Tactical & Operational Reporting

Each engagement includes detailed threat reports, real-time alerts, and mitigation strategies that align with your existing security infrastructure.

Why It Matters

Threats evolve daily — and organizations that rely only on reactive security are often caught off guard. CTI empowers you with early warning indicators, adversary tactics, and current threat trends, giving your team the context it needs to defend against sophisticated attacks before they strike.

Tools and Methodologies

I utilize sources such as open-source intelligence (OSINT), proprietary threat feeds, and dark web monitors. My intelligence process aligns with the Diamond Model of Intrusion Analysis, MITRE ATT&CK Framework, and STIX/TAXII standards to ensure structured and reliable information sharing.
