Incident Response

Contain, Investigate, Recover

Incident Response is critical in today’s threat landscape, where security breaches are often a matter of “when” not “if.” I provide structured and immediate response capabilities to contain and recover from cyber incidents quickly and effectively.

My services include 24/7 response coordination, forensic log analysis, malware identification, and root cause investigation. I work with tools like Wireshark, Volatility, and Sleuth Kit, following frameworks like NIST 800-61 and the SANS IR model to restore normal operations swiftly and securely.

Whether you're currently experiencing a breach or want to build an effective response plan, this service is ideal for minimizing downtime, preserving reputation, and protecting digital assets. Time is critical — let’s prepare and respond decisively.
