Penetration Testing

Overview

Penetration Testing Services simulate real-world cyberattacks to identify vulnerabilities in your systems, applications, or networks. Conducted in a controlled environment, my goal is to uncover weaknesses before malicious actors exploit them, providing actionable solutions to strengthen your defenses.

What I Offer

Network Testing: External/Internal assessments of firewalls, routers, and servers.
Web App Testing: API security, SQLi, XSS, and logic flaws.
Social Engineering: Phishing/pretexting simulations.
Wireless Testing: Wi-Fi and IoT device security.
Reporting: Proof-of-concept exploits + prioritized remediation steps.

Methodologies

I follow OWASP Top 10, PTES, and NIST SP 800-115 standards, using tools like Metasploit, Burp Suite, and custom Python/Bash scripts.
